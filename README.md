## 🚀 NEW RELEASE: Farming Simulator Team Sync v2.2 🚜

Tired of paying for a 24/7 dedicated server just to play with your crew? **Farming Simulator Team Sync** is a **ready-to-run desktop app (`.exe`)** that lets your team share the exact same farm for **FREE**! 🎉

It acts like a digital relay race using your favorite cloud drive (Google Drive, OneDrive, or Dropbox). When you finish your farming shift, upload your progress. When your buddy logs on, they restore it with a single click and take over right where you left off! 

**No configurations, no coding, and absolutely NO Python required!** Just download, run, and get to work! 🌾

***

### 🌟 FULL INTERACTIVE FEATURES 🌟

#### 📊 **Your Virtual Farm Dashboard**
* 🌾 **Dual-Game Support:** Fully optimized to support both **Farming Simulator 22** AND **Farming Simulator 25** seamlessly! Switch between games with a single click.
* ✨ **Slick Glassmorphism UI:** A stunning, modern dark-mode desktop interface featuring real-time stat counters for your saves, mod counts, and cloud utilization.
* 🔥 **"Latest Save" Hero Card:** The absolute newest team save file is always pinned right at the top with a massive **"Restore Latest"** quick action button.
* 💻 **Active Terminal Log:** A live, scrolling feed at the bottom of your screen that shows you exactly what the app is doing in real-time (successes, syncs, and file transfers).

#### 🚜 **Crew Management & Roles**
* 👑 **Custom Role Maker:** Hand out unique titles to your friends with custom emojis! Make someone the **🛠️ Chief Mechanic**, **🌾 Crop Manager**, or **🚜 Heavy Equipment Driver**.
* 👥 **Roster Registry:** Easily add, modify, or remove players from the active team list directly from the settings console.
* 📂 **Shared Folder Init:** Start a brand new team from scratch! The app will automatically build the entire folder framework (`/saves`, `/mods`) in your shared cloud drive with one click.

#### ⚡ **One-Click Automation (No Brainpower Required)**
* 📦 **Smart Backup & Restore:** Forget digging through Windows folders. The app automatically zips, cleans out old data, and drops the latest save straight into your game slot.
* 🧩 **Auto Mod Sync:** Instantly scans the team's shared drive against your local files, tells you exactly what you're missing, and installs it automatically!
* 🔎 **Search & Filter Engine:** Looking for a specific save? Sort by *Newest*, *Oldest*, or search for keywords in the shift notes to find exactly what you need.
* 📝 **Shift Changelogs:** Read what your teammates actually accomplished during their shift before you boot up the game.

#### 💬 **Live Discord Integration**
* 📢 **Upload Alerts:** The moment a teammate finishes their shift, a beautiful custom card is sent straight to your Discord channel showing who played, the file size, and their shift changelog!
* 🎮 **Live Game Status:** Broadcasts an alert to your server the exact second a team member launches the game tool so others know the fields are active.

#### 🎨 **Choose Your Team's Vibe**
Match your dashboard to your farming style with built-in visual themes:
* 🌾 **Farming Green** (The classic organic look)
* 🌌 **Midnight Blue** (Deep cyber aesthetic for night-shift farming)
* 🍂 **Amber Harvest** (Warm, cozy autumn tones)

1️⃣ **Download the `.exe` file** from the `#releases` channel or GitHub.
